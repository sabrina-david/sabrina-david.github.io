Terms & Conditions

Translation agency: Odysseus–Translation

1. Prices:

The rates shown on the price list are ground prices, and should be understood to be exclusive of VAT (value-added tax). Depending on the difficulty and condition of the text(s), a surcharge may also be charged. Apart from that, article 20 of the regulation for the compensation of the interpreters and translators (BStKR) also applies.

2. Lines:

The number of lines is determined in the source language (i.e. before translation). The translation fee will be charged according to the number of lines (a normal line contains 55 characters including spaces).

3. Difficulty of the Text:

General texts will be charged according to the ground prices. Texts which consist of simple composition and which contain only a limited amount of technical, legal or other special terminology are considered general texts.
Technical texts will be charged the ground price with an appropriate surcharge. These are texts that have an overall difficult composition and often contain technical terminology. The classification of the difficulty is at the discretion of the translation agency.

4. Extras:

In cases of illegible texts, flawed manuscripts, handwritings, and photocopies that are difficult to decipher, the client is obliged to provide the translation agency with all necessary support in order for the translation to be carried out successfully. An appropriate surcharge above the ground price may be necessary.

5. Orders and Delivery Dates:

The translation order must be given in writing. If desired, the order may be confirmed in writing, with a quotation by the translation agency. Additionally, the contractor is entitled to consult an expert (Art. 101 OR) in order to carry out the translation accurately. The difficulty of a text and the necessary waiting time for the translation can only be assessed after a detailed examination by the translation agency. If, due to unavoidable or unforeseen circumstances, the delivery date can not be met, the translation agency is entitled, either to withdraw from the contract or to ask for an appropriate grace time which meets the circumstance s in order to carry out the contract. If the translation is not delivered before the end of the grace period, the client is entitled to withdraw from the contract. Further rights, in particular claims for damages, are in all of these cases excluded. The contract is fulfilled with the sending of the finished translation to the designated recipient.

6. Dispatch:

Upon completion, the translation will normally be sent to the client through data transfer (E-mail). If desired the translation may also be transmitted to the client via postal service (the post) and, if necessary, on DVD. The client assumes the risk from the point of dispatch in such cases.

7. Complaints:

All translations are made to the best of our ability and are of very high standard. Should, however, against expectation, one of our translations contain some shortcomings, this should be reported to us immediately. The complaint must be launched within six weeks of delivery and it must be done in writing. Otherwise the translations would be considered as being error free, and to have met the expectations of the client and are thus fully accepted by him. In the case of inaccurate or faulty translations, the client has a claim for a new translation. We assume no liability for mistakes in the translation caused by the client through faulty or incomplete information or flawed original texts.

8. Withdrawal from the Translation Contract:

If the client withdraws from the given translation contract without due legal or contractual cause, the contractor may charge the client all actual costs accrued through the acceptance and since the acceptance of the order.

9. Confidentiality:

The translation agency will treat all information received through the contents of the translated text as confidential, so far as the information is not generally known already.

10. Terms of Payment:

The client will be charged for the translation service after its completion and delivery. There are no deductions for payment in cash. Additional payment periods may be arranged on an individual basis. The royalty must be paid within fourteen days after delivery. An interest of ten percent will charged for every delay in payment or default.

11. The place of performance and area of jurisdiction is the business location of the contractor, Switzerland.
